Value proposition

Nebula 42 creates decentralized supply chain solutions on different distributed ledger technologies, which allow monitor and possible optimization of the environmental impacts of various complex industrial supply chains. One of the measures is the embedded Greenhouse Gas (GHG) emissions, which is currently not considered and recorded during the entire product value-chain and thus not factored into the productâ€™s final price.

We will bring the solutions into the market in the following forms:

  1. Create a specific use case for the Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) market. Develop and operate a platform, and generate revenues by providing data to clients involved in spot cargo procurement.
  2. Setting up supply chains directly for organizations for a fee, including both the installation and the licenses.
  3. Public network with built-in tokens and incentive mechanisms.
  4. Provide supply chain descriptors tor the O&G, Bank, and Insurance sectors.
